Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our home and care.

What level of care is provided?
We provide 24/7 assistance with daily living activities, medication management, and specialized care plans. Our staff is trained to handle various needs with dignity and respect.
Can family visit anytime?
Absolutely. We believe family connection is vital. Visiting hours are flexible, and we encourage families to participate in meals and activities.
Is the facility secure?
Yes. Our home is equipped with modern security systems, including 24-hour monitoring and secure entry points, ensuring a safe environment for all residents.
How are meals handled?
We prepare nutritious, home-cooked meals daily, tailored to the dietary requirements and preferences of each resident. Healthy snacks are also available throughout the day.
